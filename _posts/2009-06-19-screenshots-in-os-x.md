* Entire screen: ⌘+⇧+3
* Select area with mouse: ⌘+⇧+4
* certain element (window, menu etc): ⌘+⇧+4 followed by *spacebar*
* add *ctrl* to copy screenshot to clipboard instead of creating a file on the desktop
